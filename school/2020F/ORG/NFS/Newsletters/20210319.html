March 19, 2021

Norse Film Society met yesterday to discus a few items regarding the remainder of the semester.  If you couldn't make or need a recap see below.

Shirts
We are still trying to figure out the logistics of money collection and shirt distribution, but we will make it happen if there is enough demand.  Even if you have previously expressed interest, please fill out this form if you want a shirt.

2021-22 E-Board Elections
As the year comes to a close, we will need to prepare for next year by electing our next e-board.  Positions include; President, Vice President, Treasurer, Public Relations Chair, & Secretary.  More information on each position's responsibilities can be found in our constitution.  If you're interested in running, please contact incumbent Co-President Collin Hudson by April 9th.
Candidates will give speeches at our April 9th meeting followed by voting taking place April 12th-16th voting on a different position each day.

Norse Film Fest 2021
Our final & biggest event of the year, Norse Film Fest is a celebration of everything that was made by NKU's students in the past 12 months, any NKU student may submit any short film or project done in the past year be it for a class, organization, or personally.  We highly encourage anyone who's made anything this year to submit it via this form.

The showing itself will take place live on YouTube starting April 20th at 7:30p and consist the screening followed by audience voting for best film of each category as well as an award for best film overall.

Finally, join us next week where we plan to watch something, likely an Oscar nominee.
